How to

Understanding the steps to get the most off our solution

Watch demo

1 Register and login

To get started, you'll need to register and log in to our platform. Registration is a quick and easy process, allowing you to create a personalized account. Once logged in, you'll have access to a range of powerful agricultural tools and services. Our user-friendly interface ensures that you can get started in no time.

2 You can upload the image of your soil, or crop; for analysis

Once you're logged in, you can take advantage of our image analysis feature. Whether you're concerned about your soil's fertility or need to diagnose issues with your crops, simply upload images of your soil or plants. Our AI-powered chat bot will then process these images, providing you with in-depth analysis and insights. It's a hassle-free way to gain a better understanding of your agricultural needs.

4Chat Instant feedback

With our platform, you don't have to wait for answers. As soon as you upload an image, you'll receive instant feedback. Our chat bot will quickly analyze the images and provide you with expert advice and information. This real-time feedback ensures that you can make informed decisions about your agricultural practices and take immediate steps to address any concerns. Our goal is to be your reliable agriculture companion, and this feature is designed to support you every step of the way.
